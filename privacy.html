Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Oryxen Labs ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you visit oryxen.tech or use our products, and how we use it.

1. Who we are

Oryxen Labs is an independent developer studio based in Belgium, EU. Contact: jdioses@outlook.be.

2. Data we collect

We collect minimal data:

  • Contact form: Name, email address, and message you voluntarily submit.
  • Usage data: If analytics are enabled, anonymized page views (no personal identifiers).
  • Local storage: Theme preference (dark/light) stored in your browser only — never sent to us.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising networks, or sell your data.

3. How we use your data

  • Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • We do not store form data on our servers — submissions open your email client directly (mailto).
  • Analytics data (if any) is used to understand traffic patterns and improve the site.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • GitHub — source code hosting (open-source repos are publicly visible).
  • GitHub Pages — static site hosting for this website.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

To exercise any right, contact us at jdioses@outlook.be. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data retention

Email correspondence is retained only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry. Theme preferences are stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time via browser settings.

7. Security

This site is served over HTTPS. We use GitHub Pages as hosting, which follows industry-standard security practices. No passwords or payment data are collected.

8. Third-party links

Our site links to GitHub and the Google Play Store. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

9. Children

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at jdioses@outlook.be.
